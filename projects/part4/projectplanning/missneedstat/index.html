Mission Need Statement

“Take an image of Earth from orbit every minute for 24 hours and send the data and the system’s status back to Earth to be processed by a ground station”.

The mission need statement is like the project objective statement but describes the intended use of the CubeSat. To be as specific as possible the exact number and frequency of images were outlined as well as where the images will be taken from. This statement will be used to outline the requirements and functions that will need to be performed by the CubeSat. Some things that can be inferred from this statement are that the CubeSat will need to operate in space conditions, have long-distance communications to the ground station, be able to transmit the image fast enough to be ready to transmit another one in 60 seconds, and be powered for at least 24 hours, likely with a safety margin.
