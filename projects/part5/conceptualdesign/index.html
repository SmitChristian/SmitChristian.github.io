Conceptual Design Phase

This section will detail the preliminary conceptual design phase of this project. Based on the list of requirements, a risk map, design option tree, and trade off table were developed. The risk map will identify the possible risks as well as their likelihood of occurrence. Multiple risk maps were developed for each conceptual design proposed. The design option tree provides a visual aid for determining concepts based on system. Multiple design option trees were made to represent each system and the decisions that can be made regarding how they will function. The trade off table provides a visual/mathematical aid for choosing one of the concepts proposed. The table parameters were determined by the project group based on the list of requirements and their importance.
