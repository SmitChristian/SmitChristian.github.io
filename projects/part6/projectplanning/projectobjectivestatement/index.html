Project Objective Statement

Add data to this page

“Design and construct a CubeSat for $1250 to take and transmit images to a ground station for 24 hours with 4 students in 14 weeks”.

The project objective statement is short, often a sentence, and describes the expected result of a project and available resources and time to do so. The resources are the people and money available for this project, which will be the four students in this group and the $1250 provided by the university. Future funding beyond $1250 may be secured if the project is expected to run over-budget. The time is limited by the length of the semester until reading day which is when the project will be presented. A CubeSat (short for cube satellite) is a small satellite that typically has small, short-term objectives. The one being designed will take pictures and send them to ground stations.
